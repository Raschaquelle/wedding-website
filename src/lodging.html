Description
The first floor of the home features a large open concept kitchen, dining, and living room perfect for family gatherings. It includes a large flat screen smart TV, a gas fireplace, and a large couch with plenty of room for extra seating and lounging. As you pass through the living room you're met by two large double sliding doors that lead out to a large deck with a comfortable and spacious outdoor seating area. Also on the top floor is the master suite. The master bath includes a large, deep soaking tub, large walk in shower, massive walk in closet and private toilet room. The top floor also includes a guest bathroom and a laundry room equipped with a brand new top of the line full capacity Whirlpool washer and dryer set.
As you venture down to the bottom level of the home you'll find an additional family living area with another couch and smart TV. The downstairs room configuration is as follows: Queen Suite, twin room with 2 twin beds, and a bunk bed room with two twin bunk beds. In addition, the downstairs couch pulls out into a bed for additional sleeping space. Beyond the lower level living area is the bottom back patio equipped with a brand new 4 seater hot tub.
