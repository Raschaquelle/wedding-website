Covid-related Information for Ben + Rachelle's Wedding
COVID Related Expectations for the Wedding
  • We feel responsible for making sure the risks are as low as possible and that everyone feels safe.
    • will be monitoring the situation as it evolves and will make adjustments as necessary, but are planning conservatively and do not expect significant improvements prior to the wedding
  • People will be attending from various locations in Colorado, the DC metro area, rural Wyoming, and western NY
    • We understand that this presents a reasonable exposure risk and thus we are expecting all participants to follow the pre-travel and travel requirements in the following section.
  • The pre-travel and travel requirements are to allow us to stay in shared lodging together and have as close to a “normal” experience as possible
    • We are not requiring masks or social distancing during the wedding and wedding-related festivities
  • We expect and trust all attendees to be respectful of our requirements (as well as any local, state, or national COVID related requirements)
  • If you have questions or concerns about these requirements, please ask
COVID Related Travel Requirements & Information
Updated as of 01/21/2021
  • Pre-travel requirements
    • Reasonable self-isolation for 2 weeks prior to arrival (travel time can be included): Basically not seeing anyone without both parties wearing a mask unless:
      • You live with them and they also are following these guidelines
    • Going to work, healthcare appointments, grocery store, etc. are fine as long as masks are required to be worn by all parties
  • Travel requirements
    • No flying as it is too much of an exposure risk
    • Wear a mask inside any public building (i.e. gas station)
    • Lodging during travel can include:
      • Camping (if at a campground, maintain social distancing)
      • Airbnb/hotel/motel if it has a mask policy and separate facilities from other guests (i.e. not a shared kitchen/bathroom in an Airbnb)
