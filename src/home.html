Our Story

While we technically met in the fall of 2017 in grad school, our story didn’t really begin until a trip to Great Sand Dunes National Park the following semester. That first outdoor adventure was followed by many many more as we fell in love with the Rocky Mountains and each other. Travels close to home were just as important though, as the train rides to campus allowed us to share our passion for science, engineering, and technology. It turned out we also shared a love of board games and playing together became an integral part of our relationship, so much so that we’re currently designing a game together. We got engaged exactly where it all began, the Great Sand Dunes, and are really excited to continue our adventure together.
